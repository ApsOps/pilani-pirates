This guide will show you how to download from The Pilani Pirates.

There are many different DC clients, this guide explains how to use Apex DC++.

Apex DC ++

Installing

  1. Download Apex DC++
  2. Install it.

Download

  1. Go to 127.0.0.1:8000 and select a download. Click "Get this file".
  2. Now your web browser might ask you what application you want to open the file with. Select Apex DC++ and make sure the browser remembers your choice.
  3. Your files are now being downloaded in Apex DC++.

Problems, questions?

Q: Magnets don't open in my DC client.
A: The easiest way to fix this is to uninstall and then install Apex DC++ again.
Or fix your settings.

Other info

Always share as much as you can and keep your DC client running as long as you can. Also, try to upload at least as much as you download.
Thats the way to keep the DC network alive.
